Privacy Policy

Last Changed: June 30, 2021

This privacy policy refers to the website: retorn.rocks and the browser extension: ReTorn

Data Collection

ReTorn does not currently collect usage information from users, but may collect usage information using Google Analytics in future revisions to gain insight on what features are being utilized and to gain a general understanding of how the extension is used. Google Analytics data will only be shared with the developers of ReTorn. We do not share or sell this information to third-parties. If you are uncomfortable with sharing this information with the developers, you may opt-out by removing the extension from your browser.

All other data used is stored locally within the browser.

ReTorn may share your Torn API key with Torn Stats for third-party features only after getting explicit permission from the user to do so.

ReTorn does not collect your API key.

By using the ReTorn browser extension, you accept this policy.
