Privacy Policy

This privacy policy refers to the website: retorn.rocks and the browser extension: ReTorn

Data Collection

The developers of ReTorn do not collect information from users. The ReTorn browser extension saves extension settings information in the Sync storage of the browser. Users that are logged in to the browser have the option to sync data to their browser account so that it may be synced to the browser on other devices they login to. This information is not shared with the developers of ReTorn.

All other data used is stored locally within the browser. This includes your Torn API data and API key.

ReTorn may share your Torn API key with Torn Stats for third-party features only after getting explicit permission from the user to do so. You can review Torn Stats Terms of Service here: https://www.tornstats.com/tos

ReTorn does not collect or sell your API key or API data.

We do not share any information with third-parties, other than what has been stated above. We do not sell any information to third-parties. If you are uncomfortable with this policy, you may opt-out by removing the extension from your browser.
