Privacy Policy

Last Changed: July 27, 2022

This privacy policy refers to the website: retorn.rocks and the browser extension: ReTorn

Data Collection

The developers of ReTorn do not collect information from users. The ReTorn browser extension saves extension settings information in the Chrome Sync storage of the Chrome browser. Users that are logged in to the Chrome browser with their Google account have the option to sync data to their Google account so that it may be synced to the Chrome browser on other devices they login to. This information is not shared with the developers of ReTorn.

All other data used is stored locally within the browser.

ReTorn may share your Torn API key with Torn Stats for third-party features only after getting explicit permission from the user to do so.

ReTorn does not collect or sell your API key.

We do not share any information to third-parties, other than what has been stated above. We do not sell any information to third-parties. If you are uncomfortable with this policy, you may opt-out by removing the extension from your browser.

By using the ReTorn browser extension, you accept this policy.
